Installing Sana Client

Installing...

Open file explorer by pressing the folder icon at the bottom of your screen

once opened go to the "Downloads" tab on your left side

then locate Sana_"Version"

right click it then extract it

Once it's done extracting, open the new folder named Sana_"Version"

Then open the launcher and your done
